Spoke & Rail is a route planner for combining cycling and transit. Google Maps doesn't have this as an option. This does.

Enter any two points and it shows you the fastest way to get there using your bike and public transit together - carrying your bike on the train, locking it at the station, or using bikeshare for the first or last mile. It covers Philadelphia (SEPTA + PATCO + Indego), Boston (MBTA), and New York City (MTA + Metro-North + NJ Transit).

Found a bug or a wrong fare? Send a note.
